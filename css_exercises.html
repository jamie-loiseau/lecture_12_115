Pucca!

the mexican trash dog

Pucca was born in Mexico. She was abandoned in a trash can with her siblings. When she was found, she was the only one alive. My family then adopted her. She grew up to be the most amazing, loyal and playful dog ever. She loves food and walks. She loves playing with her ladybug toy that looks like its way past its expiration date. Nobody is sure of what she sees in it anymore. (I think she keeps it for emotional support.)


Pucca has a very strange personality but thats what makes her even better! She goes crazy for a cat thats never shown any interest in her. She loves to sleep in the most outrageous and uncomfortable positions. Shes obsessed with eating animals outside which is the only downside to her. When she gets bored, she licks her paws until they bleed. again, shes a very strange dog.


she LOVES posing for pictures though!

Thank you for reading about the greatest dog in the history of dogs!
